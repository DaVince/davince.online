# General progress

## Unlock Dad's Note/Home

- Get the knfe
  - Go to Downpour and get knife piece 1
  - Go to Mines and get knife piece 2
  - Alternatively, use a sharp key or cracked orb
- Stab the door, go to Mausoleum/Gehenna
- Takes you to Corpse
- Defeat Mother in the Corpse (unlocks Dad's Note)

## Beat Home

New run: [get Dad's Note](https://bindingofisaacrebirth.wiki.gg/wiki/Dad%27s_Note)
- Go through A Strange Door in the starting room of Depths 2, Necropolis 2 or Dank Depths
- Takes you to special Mausoleum/Gehenna 2.
- Defeat the boss, it gives you Dad's Note
- Begin the [Ascent](https://bindingofisaacrebirth.wiki.gg/wiki/Ascent)
- Go all the way up to Home
- Open Mom's Chest to unlock Red Key and Cracked Key
- Defeat The Beast


# Other goals

- [Tainted characters](https://bindingofisaacrebirth.wiki.gg/wiki/Tainted_Characters): use red key, cracked key or Soul of Cain on the hall before Mom's Bedroom in Home
- [Horse pills](https://bindingofisaacrebirth.wiki.gg/wiki/Pills#Horse_Pills): defeat Mega Satan as Tainted Eve
- [Cracked orb](https://bindingofisaacrebirth.wiki.gg/wiki/Cracked_Orb): defeat The Beast as Eve
- [Soul of Cain](https://bindingofisaacrebirth.wiki.gg/wiki/Soul_of_Cain): defeat Hush and Boss Rush as Tainted Cain
